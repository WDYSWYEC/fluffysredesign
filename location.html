Kevyn Lee and Darren Wellington are the founders and owners of Fluffy’s Sno-Balls: A New Orleans Original. Kevyn have been making quality gourmet sno-balls since the age of 12, at his grandmother’s local store in his hometown of New Orleans, where he was born and raised. In the middle of the 2021 Covid-19 pandemic they worked tirelessly to ensure that they would provide the best products to their customers. They spent 4 months specifically formulating, at the start each of the 35+ flavors. The quality and consistency of the flavors was so important to them that they had panels of native New Orleanians taste-test and validate the accuracy of flavors. With full panel approvals, the pair knew it was time to execute and bring the product to market. Since opening their doors, Fluffy’s Sno-Balls has increased their flavor options from 35 flavors to well over 55+ flavor options. All Fluffy’s Sno-Balls are made with high quality ingredients, pure cane sugar, or aspartame and all products are recyclable and biodegradable. Additionally, all products offered at Fluffy’s Sno-Balls are directly imported from the City of New Orleans.
